Advanced stats

The NBA's analytics revolution has changed the way we think about basketball,

the metrics used to analyze and predict games can be found through Basketball-Reference, HoopData, NBA.com, or ESPN

This dashboard will give us a look at advanced stats per team, per player, group them and try to predict games

  • Win record, this one is self-explanatory
  • Loss record, ditto.
  • MOV : Margin of victory, avg over a whole season
  • SOS : Strenght of schedule
  • ORTg : Offensive Rating; an estimate of points produced per 100 posessions
  • DRTg : Defensive Rating; an estimate of points allowed per 100 posessions
  • FTr : Free throw attempt rate; number of FT attempts per FG (field goal) attempt
  • 3PAr : Three point attempt rate; percentage of 3-point shots taken
  • OEFGPct : Offensive effective field goal percentage;
  • ORB : Offensive rebound percentage
  • DEFGPct : Defensive effective field goal percentage;
  • RDB : Defensive rebound percentage
